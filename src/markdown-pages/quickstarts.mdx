<Intro>

Start building out your observability story with the community resources found in Quickstarts. Quickly import great Dashboards, Alerts and Synthetic scripts into your New Relic accounts.

</Intro>

Follow this document to install the Quickstart nerdlet as well as learn how to add your own Dashboards, Alerts and Synthetic scripts to grow the library

## Subscribing to the Quickstarts Nerdlet

Subscribe accounts to Quickstarts.

To learn about the appropriate permissions for subscribing, see [Permissions for managing applications](/build-apps/permission-manage-apps).

<Steps>

<Step>

Browse to the `Apps` section in New Relic One

[Click here to open the New Relic Apps view](https://one.newrelic.com/launcher/nr1-core.home?pane=eyJuZXJkbGV0SWQiOiJucjEtY29yZS5ob21lLXNjcmVlbiJ9&overlay=eyJuZXJkbGV0SWQiOiJjYXRhbG9nLmFwcC1kaXJlY3RvcnkifQ==)

</Step>

<Step>

1. Select Quickstarts from the list of applications in the New Relic One Catalog.
2. Click **Add this app**. Note that this button says **Manage access** if the app has already been subscribed to an account you manage.
3. On the **Account access** page listing the accounts you can subscribe to an application:

- Select the accounts you want to subscribe Quickstarts to.
- Click the update button. Now you and members of the accounts you have subscribed to Quickstarts can launch it from New Relic One.

</Step>

</Steps>
